A field is enclosed by a wall \(AH\) and fencing at the rest of the boundary. Given: $EF=20$ $m$, $BC=y$ $m$, $AB=DC=2x$ $m$, $DE=3x$ $m$; the total fencing length (excluding the wall) is $130$ m; the area of $HBCG$ is $3$ times that of $DEFG$. Find the length $HF$.
\end{columns}
$\begin{array}{rcl}
\only<handout:1|1>{\text{Fence length}&=&130 m\\
2\cdot 2x+2y+2\cdot 3x +20&=&130\\
10x+2y&=&110\\
5x+y&=&55\\}
y&=&55-5x\\
Area(HBCG)&=&3\cdot Area(DEFH)\\
\only<handout:1|1>{y\cdot(2x+20)&=&3\cdot3x\cdot 20\\
(55-5x)(2x+20)-180x&=&0\\
110x+1100-10x^2-100x-180x&=&0\\
-10x^2-170x+1100&=&0\\}
x^2+17x-110&=&0\\
(x-5)(x+22)&=&0\\

x=5&\text{or}&\fcCancel{2}{x=-22}, \quad x>0\\
y&=&55-5x=55-5\cdot 5= 30\\
HF&=&(y+3x)m=(30+3\cdot 5)m= 45 m
\end{array}
$
