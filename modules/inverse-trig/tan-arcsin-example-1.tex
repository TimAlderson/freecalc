Find $\displaystyle
\tan \left( \Arcsin \left( \frac{1}{3}\right) \right)
$.
\begin{itemize}
\item<2->  Let $\theta = \Arcsin \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)$, so $\sin \theta = \frac{ \alertNoH{3}{1}}{\alertNoH{4}{3}}$.
\item<3->  Draw a right triangle with \alertNoH{3}{opposite side $1$} and \alertNoH{4}{hypotenuse $3$}.
\item<5-> Let the angle $\theta$ be as labeled. \uncover<6->{ Then $ \alertNoH{6,7}{\sin\theta= \frac{1 }{3}}$ \uncover<7->{and so $\alertNoH{7}{ \theta= \arcsin\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)}$.}}
\item<8->  \alertNoH{8-9}{Length of adjacent side $ =\worksheet{ \fcAnswer{9}{\sqrt{3^2-1^2}} \uncover<10->{ = \sqrt{8} = 2\sqrt{2}.}}$}
\item<11->  Then \alertNoH{ 11-12}{$\tan \left(\Arcsin \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)\right) = \fcAnswer{12}{ \frac{1}{ 2\sqrt{2}}.}$}
\end{itemize}
